Privacy Policy

VP3D Dashboard (internal application of VantagePoint 3D & Media) — Effective July 10, 2026

What this application is

The VP3D Dashboard is an internal business dashboard used exclusively by VantagePoint 3D & Media staff. It is not offered to the public and has no external users or customers.

What data it accesses

With authorization from the company owner, the dashboard reads business data from services the company already uses, including QuickBooks Online (financial reports such as profit and loss and accounts receivable), our booking system, and our customer relationship manager. Access to QuickBooks Online is granted through Intuit's OAuth authorization flow and can be revoked at any time from the QuickBooks account settings or by disconnecting within Intuit's app management tools.

How data is used and stored

Data is displayed to authenticated company staff for internal reporting only. Financial data is visible only to the company owner role. OAuth tokens are stored server-side in access-controlled storage. Report data is cached in memory for up to 15 minutes and is not otherwise retained. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.
